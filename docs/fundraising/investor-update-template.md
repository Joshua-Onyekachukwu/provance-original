## Usage Notes

### Cadence
- **During active fundraising:** Weekly updates with key metrics + next steps
- **Post-close (first 6 months):** Monthly updates
- **Post-close (6+ months):** Quarterly updates + annual board meeting

### Tone
- Honest and direct. Bad news early is better than bad news late.
- Share proportionally: good metrics get space, challenges get candor.
- Investors are partners — treat them like extended team members.

### What Not to Include
- Raw PII of customers or employees
- Unrealistic projections that weren't discussed
- Competitor criticism (focus on your own execution)
- Information that would harm the company if leaked
