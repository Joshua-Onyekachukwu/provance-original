## 1. Phase 1.0 Beta Program → Paid Transition

### Program Structure
The Phase 1.0 Beta Program is a **gated design partner program** for 20 organizations across 4 segments (Journalism, Legal, Trust & Safety, Developers). Beta participants receive 6 months free at the highest applicable tier in exchange for active usage, feedback, and case study participation.

### Conversion Targets

| From | To | Target Rate | Reasoning |
|------|----|-------------|-----------|
| Beta Journalism | **Pro** | **40%** | Individual journalists need solo accounts |
| Beta Journalism | **Team** | **20%** | Newsroom desks need shared workspace |
| Beta Legal | Team or Enterprise | 30% Team, 10% Enterprise | Law firms have team/enterprise budgets |
| Beta Trust & Safety | Enterprise | 25% | Enterprise is natural fit for platform teams |
| Beta Developers | API Pro | 40% | Developers need programmatic access |
| Public Free Trial | Pro | 15–20% | Standard SaaS conversion benchmark |
| Public Free Trial | Team | 3–5% | Teams discover via individual upgrades |

### Conversion Pricing Incentives

| Converting From | To | Std Price | Beta Price (6mo) | Discount |
|----------------|----|-----------|-----------------|----------|
| Beta Journalism | Pro | $49/mo | $37/mo | 25% |
| Beta Journalism | Team | $249/mo | $199/mo | 20% |
| Beta Legal | Team | $249/mo | $199/mo | 20% |
| Beta Trust & Safety | Enterprise | $3,000/mo | $2,500/mo | ~17% |
| Beta Developers | API Pro | $199/mo | $179/mo | 10% |

## 3. Key Financial Milestones for Seed Round

| # | Milestone | Metric | Target Date | Investor Significance |
|---|-----------|--------|-------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Design partner activation** | 15+ active partners | M3 (Sep 2026) | Product-market fit validated |
| 2 | **First paid conversion** | 5+ beta→paid customers | M5 (Nov 2026) | Willingness to pay confirmed |
| 3 | **First enterprise deal** | 1 at $2K+/mo | M6 (Dec 2026) | Enterprise sales motion works |
| 4 | **$10K MRR** | Monthly recurring revenue | M9 (Mar 2027) | Revenue model at scale |
| 5 | **20% trial conversion** | Industry benchmark | M12 (Jun 2027) | Funnel efficiency validated |
| 6 | **$300K ARR run rate** | For Series A readiness | M18 (Dec 2027) | Ready for institutional capital |

### Seed Round Close Triggers

**Minimum close trigger:** 5+ paid conversions from beta + 1 enterprise contract (M5–M6)
**Target close trigger:** $10K+ MRR + 20+ paying customers (M9)
**Latest close:** Before cash runway drops below 6 months (M10–M12)
