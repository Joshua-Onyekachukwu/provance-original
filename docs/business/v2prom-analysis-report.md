# V2PROM Analysis Report

## Scoring Model

Scores are out of 10 and reflect the current documented state, not a fully built company.

## Overall Score

`7.1 / 10`

This is a strong concept with a credible strategic wedge, but execution risk remains high because the product is still pre-build and the business systems are not yet operational.

## Category Scores

### Vision: 8.8

Strong category-level vision around trust infrastructure and explainable verification.

### Value: 8.2

High value for trust-sensitive customers, especially legal, media, and enterprise verification teams.

### Viability: 6.6

Viability is promising but constrained by dataset requirements, GTM complexity, and high trust burden.

### Product: 7.0

Product concept is coherent, but MVP scope must stay disciplined to avoid overreach.

### Revenue: 6.8

Multiple monetization paths exist, but pricing and segment sequencing require more validation.

### Operations: 6.1

Operational maturity is currently low because the company system, team model, and governance are still being defined.

### Market: 7.9

Market pressure is real and increasing, but incumbents already exist. Positioning must stay differentiated.

## Strengths

- Strong narrative around explainability and evidence
- High-value use cases beyond generic consumer detection
- Compounding moat via fingerprint database and attribution
- Clear strategic wedge in legal-grade and professional workflows

## Weaknesses

- No implementation yet
- Dataset dependency is significant
- Naming and brand architecture are unresolved
- Current materials mix consumer, enterprise, and legal motions too early
- Legal-grade positioning may outpace proof if launched carelessly

## Risks

- False positives erode credibility
- Trust-sensitive buyers require stronger proof than average SaaS buyers
- Video can become a cost sink before core image PMF is reached
- Broad positioning may dilute the sharpest wedge

## Opportunities

- Own the evidence and workflow layer rather than just detection
- Build a standards-aligned reputation through transparent benchmarking
- Monetize team and enterprise workflows with stronger ACV than consumer plans
- Expand into platform API and provenance-aware infrastructure

## Recommendations To Raise Score

### Move 1

Narrow launch ICP to professional verification users. This improves viability and revenue quality.

### Move 2

Publish a benchmark and methodology posture early. This improves product trust and market credibility.

### Move 3

Prioritize evidence UX and report workflows beside image detection MVP. This improves value and positioning.

### Move 4

Introduce a formal dataset, labeling, and model governance program. This improves operations and viability.

### Move 5

Resolve company/product naming before launch. This improves market clarity and conversion.
