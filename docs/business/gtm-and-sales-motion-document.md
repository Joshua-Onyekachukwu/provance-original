# GTM And Sales Motion Document

## Objective

Define how Provance gets from concept to initial revenue with a focused, trust-oriented go-to-market strategy.

## Core GTM Principle

Do not launch as a broad consumer AI detector.

Launch as a **high-trust verification product** for users who:

- have repeat verification needs
- care about explainability
- can pay for better evidence

## ICP Priority Order

### ICP 1: Journalists And Verification Teams

- pain: verifying suspicious media quickly and credibly
- urgency: high
- budget size: moderate
- value of evidence: high

### ICP 2: Investigative And Legal-Adjacent Workflows

- pain: evidence must be explainable and traceable
- urgency: high in active cases
- budget size: moderate to high
- value of report export: very high

### ICP 3: Enterprise Trust, Risk, And Fraud Teams

- pain: deepfake fraud, impersonation, and media abuse
- urgency: high
- budget size: high
- value of API and workflow controls: very high

### ICP 4: Developers

- pain: building verification internally is expensive
- urgency: medium
- budget size: variable
- value of API: high

## Positioning For GTM

### Primary Message

Verify suspicious media with evidence, explanation, and attribution, not just a score.

### Secondary Message

Provance helps professionals make defensible decisions when media authenticity matters.

## Sales Motion By Segment

## Motion 1: Founder-Led Design Partner Sales

Best for:

- journalists
- legal-adjacent users
- early enterprise buyers

Approach:

- identify 30-50 target organizations
- run direct outreach
- offer pilot access in exchange for workflow feedback
- convert pilots into paid early adopter plans

## Motion 2: Credibility-Led Inbound

Best for:

- researchers
- journalists
- developers

Approach:

- benchmark reports
- methodology content
- thought leadership
- sample forensic report
- landing page conversion

## Motion 3: Product-Led API Inbound

Best for:

- developers
- technical teams

Approach:

- clear docs
- limited free trial
- easy API key issuance
- webhook support

## Motion 4: Targeted Enterprise Outbound

Best for:

- trust and safety
- fraud teams
- media platforms

Approach:

- pain-specific messaging
- demo-led outreach
- custom proof-of-concept
- enterprise pricing and support

## Packaging Strategy

### Free Trial

- limited scans
- no API
- watermark or reduced report access

### Pro

- individual plan
- better scan limits
- result history
- explanation layer

### Team

- collaboration
- report export
- shared workspace

### Enterprise

- API
- security controls
- SLA
- custom usage terms

## Pricing Philosophy

- price to trust and workflow value
- avoid cheap commodity positioning
- do not compete on lowest cost per scan
- keep consumer-style pricing secondary to professional value capture

## Demand Generation Channels

- benchmark and methodology publications
- direct outreach to pilot targets
- founder content on media trust and synthetic evidence
- legal-tech and media-tech communities
- AI safety, trust, and security conferences
- SEO for verification intent terms

## Sales Funnel

1. awareness through trust content
2. credibility through sample report and methodology
3. conversion to demo or waitlist
4. pilot usage
5. paid conversion
6. expansion into team or enterprise plan

## What Converts Best

- sample forensic report
- concrete examples of explainability
- visible uncertainty discipline
- API + workflow readiness
- real case studies

## Pilot Strategy

### Goal

Secure early proof from users who create credible references.

### Pilot Structure

- 30 to 60 day pilot
- defined review workflow
- success criteria agreed upfront
- testimonial or case study rights where possible

## Metrics To Track

- demo request rate
- pilot conversion rate
- paid conversion rate
- time from first meeting to pilot
- time from pilot to paid
- usage frequency by segment
- retention by account type

## Risks

- too much focus on broad awareness before trust is earned
- too much consumer traffic with low monetization
- over-complex enterprise motion before product proof

## Recommendation

The first GTM motion should be:

1. founder-led pilot acquisition
2. credibility-led landing page and content
3. documentation-led API readiness

This gives Provance both proof and distribution without pretending to be a mass-market product too early.
