# Business Plan

## Executive Summary

Provance is building a verification platform for the AI-content era. The first product, currently framed as VerifAI, helps professionals and organizations determine whether digital media is synthetic, manipulated, or likely authentic using explainable forensic analysis, attribution, and reporting workflows.

The company should enter the market through high-trust use cases where evidence quality matters more than novelty or mass consumer reach.

## Company Overview

### Company Purpose

Build trust infrastructure for synthetic media verification.

### Initial Offering

Image verification platform with evidence-backed results, report export, and API access.

### Expansion Path

Video verification, team workflows, enterprise controls, provenance integrations, and ecosystem partnerships.

## Problem And Opportunity

Synthetic media quality is improving, but reliable verification workflows remain fragmented, opaque, and hard to operationalize. This creates opportunity in:

- journalism and media verification
- legal and evidentiary workflows
- enterprise trust and safety
- fraud and impersonation prevention

## Market Strategy

### Initial ICP Order

1. journalists and verification professionals
2. legal-adjacent and investigative workflows
3. enterprise trust and safety teams
4. developers integrating verification
5. consumers

### Go-To-Market Motion

- credibility-led content and methodology
- targeted pilot programs
- sample report and benchmark assets
- founder-led sales for early enterprise design partners
- self-serve funnel for developers and prosumers

## Product Strategy

- Start with image verification.
- Build evidence UX as a first-class differentiator.
- Expand into attribution, reports, and API before broad consumer scale.
- Introduce video only when architecture, economics, and quality are ready.

## Revenue Model

- self-serve subscription
- team plan
- enterprise contract
- usage-based API pricing
- premium report exports

## Operating Plan

### Year 1 Focus

- prove trust in MVP workflow
- secure pilot users
- establish benchmark credibility
- convert first paying teams and API customers

### Year 2 Focus

- deepen product-market fit
- improve attribution and reporting
- expand enterprise controls
- formalize retraining and model ops

## Team Plan

### Initial Core Team

- founder / product architect
- ML engineer
- full-stack engineer
- backend / platform engineer
- product designer

### Later Additions

- customer success / solutions
- GTM lead
- security / compliance support

## Risks

- quality risk from dataset insufficiency
- positioning drift
- slow sales cycles in legal and enterprise segments
- infrastructure cost shocks from video

## Strategic Roadmap

- Phase 1: foundation and benchmark readiness
- Phase 2: core image platform
- Phase 3: monetization and attribution
- Phase 4: evidence and video optimization
- Phase 5: enterprise and ecosystem scale

## Success Metrics

- trusted pilot adoption
- first recurring revenue
- retention of professional users
- improved benchmark performance over time
- gross margin discipline
