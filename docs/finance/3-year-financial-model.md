## 10. Scenario Analysis

| Scenario | Probability | Y3 ARR | Peak Burn | Breakeven | Implication |
|----------|------------|--------|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| **Conservative** | 40% | $6.7M | $636K | M24 | Seed sufficient; may need bridge to be safe |
| **Base** | 40% | $7.3M | $636K | M22 | $3M seed covers to profitability |
| **Aggressive** | 20% | $10.2M | $750K | M18 | Could raise Series A on stronger metrics |

### Conservative Case Assumptions
- Pro conversion: 12% (vs 15–20% base)
- Enterprise: 5 contracts Y1 (vs 10 base)
- Churn: 50% higher across all tiers
- API growth: 30% slower

### Aggressive Case Assumptions
- Pro conversion: 25%
- Enterprise: 15 contracts Y1 with larger average deal ($5K/mo)
- Team adoption driven by newsroom contracts
- API volume 50% higher
