### 3.1 The 'Honesty Gap'
Standard models forced a binary 'Real' or 'Fake' choice for nearly every asset. In contrast, the Provance algorithm correctly identified **0** assets as **Inconclusive**. This reduces the risk of newsrooms citing false verification results.

### 3.2 Error Analysis: Confident Wrong
We identified 4 cases where the standard model was highly confident (score > 80% or < 20%) but incorrect.
- **Failures on MJ v6 / Flux.1**: Standard models often missed high-fidelity synthetic textures.
- **Failures on Compressed Authentic**: Standard models flagged heavy JPEG artifacts as generative noise.

Provance's use of **Metadata Forensics (20%)** and **C2PA (10%)** provided the necessary weight to downgrade these suspicious visual signals to 'Authentic' or 'Inconclusive' when no generative fingerprint was found.
