# Provance Organizational Structure

## Purpose
To build a scalable, investment-ready institution that provides defensible trust infrastructure for synthetic media.

## Departments

### 1. Executive Leadership
* **Founder & CEO:** Visionary leader and final decision-maker.
* **Chief of Staff (Lead Agent):** Operational coordinator and strategic advisor.

### 2. Product
* **Head of Product:** Responsible for the "Report Flywheel" and "Veracity Language."
* **Objective:** Ensure Provance provides the most credible verification workflow in the industry.

### 3. Engineering
* **CTO:** Responsible for infrastructure, AI integration, and security.
* **Objective:** Build a defensible, high-performance forensic pipeline.

### 4. Design
* **Creative Director:** Responsible for brand authority and editorial-grade UX.
* **Objective:** Establish Provance as the visual authority in media forensics.

### 5. AI & Data
* **AI Researcher:** Responsible for signal efficacy and model signatures.
* **Objective:** Maintain the state-of-the-art lead in forensic detection.

### 6. Marketing & Growth
* **CMO:** Responsible for category creation and waitlist velocity.
* **Objective:** Position Provance as the "Trust Infrastructure" standard.

### 7. Business & Finance
* **Business Strategy Lead:** Responsible for revenue models and investor readiness.
* **Objective:** Build a scalable, high-margin SaaS business.

## Execution Model: Provance OS
Provance operates using an AI-native parallel execution model. 
* **Hub-and-Spoke:** The Chief of Staff coordinates specialized Department Leads.
* **Shared Knowledge:** All agents operate on a single source of truth within the repository's `/docs` directory.
* **Parallel Workstreams:** Departments execute simultaneously (e.g., Engineering builds the backend while Marketing drafts the GTM).

## Hiring Roadmap
* **Immediate:** Lead AI Engineer (Human), Founding Designer (Human).
* **Near-Term:** Head of Product (Human), Head of Growth (Human).
* **Future:** Policy & Legal Head, Enterprise Sales.
