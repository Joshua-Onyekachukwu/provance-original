# Data Room Checklist

## Purpose

This checklist defines the investor data room Provance should prepare before a serious external fundraising process.

## Folder Structure

1. Corporate
2. Finance
3. Product
4. Technology
5. Market And Competition
6. Customers And GTM
7. Legal
8. Team

## 1. Corporate

- company overview
- incorporation records
- founder stock issuance records
- cap table
- board or founder resolutions
- organizational chart

## 2. Finance

- financial model framework
- budget
- cash runway model
- hiring plan
- revenue assumptions
- use of funds

## 3. Product

- master product blueprint
- PRD
- feature specs
- roadmap
- sample report screenshots or mockups
- landing page plan

## 4. Technology

- system design document
- technical architecture document
- TRD
- algorithms and intelligence layer
- security posture summary
- model benchmarking plan

## 5. Market And Competition

- market thesis
- competition analysis
- positioning report
- ICP definitions
- differentiation narrative

## 6. Customers And GTM

- GTM and sales motion document
- pilot target list
- design partner pipeline
- pricing strategy
- any LOIs, pilots, or user interviews

## 7. Legal

- IP assignment agreements
- SAFE template
- privacy / terms drafts
- risk and compliance framework

## 8. Team

- team operating structure
- role descriptions
- hiring plan
- founder bio and credibility summary

## Minimum Data Room For Pre-Seed

- pitch deck
- investment memo
- fundraising strategy and valuation memo
- cap table framework
- company formation status
- product and technical docs
- roadmap
- market / competition report

## Minimum Data Room For Seed

Everything above, plus:

- benchmark results
- customer evidence
- pilot results
- live product demo
- financial model with scenario detail
- legal cleanup and signed docs

## Rule

The data room should prove:

- the problem is real
- the wedge is differentiated
- the team can execute
- the money has a clear use
- the valuation is defensible
