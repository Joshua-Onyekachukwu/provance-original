# Landing Page Strategy, Copy, And Conversion Blueprint

## Goal

The landing page should do four jobs:

1. establish trust
2. explain differentiation fast
3. capture qualified demand
4. support fundraising credibility

## Why The Landing Page Matters

For Provance, the landing page is not optional.

It is needed for:

- investor credibility
- pilot acquisition
- waitlist and demo capture
- SEO
- communicating why the product is different

## Primary Conversion Goals

- request demo
- join waitlist
- view sample report
- explore API / docs

## Primary Audience

- journalists and verification teams
- investigators and legal-adjacent users
- enterprise trust and fraud teams
- developers

## Homepage Structure

1. hero
2. trust bar
3. problem and stakes
4. how it works
5. why Provance is different
6. use cases
7. sample output / report preview
8. pricing preview
9. FAQ
10. final CTA

## Hero Copy

### Headline Options

- Verify media with evidence, not guesswork.
- Synthetic media verification for teams that need answers they can defend.
- Detect AI-generated media with explainable forensic evidence.

### Recommended Hero

**Headline:**  
Verify media with evidence, not guesswork.

**Subheadline:**  
Provance helps journalists, investigators, legal teams, and enterprise trust teams determine whether media is authentic, synthetic, or uncertain using explainable forensic analysis.

**Primary CTA:**  
Request Demo

**Secondary CTA:**  
View Sample Report

## Trust Bar

Use placeholders initially for:

- built for high-trust workflows
- explainable results
- attribution roadmap
- report-ready outputs

Later replace with:

- pilot logos
- media / partner logos
- benchmark or press references

## Problem Section Copy

### Headline

Most media detectors give you a score. They do not give you something you can defend.

### Body

When authenticity matters, a black-box score is not enough. Teams need evidence, reasoning, and outputs they can use in editorial, investigative, fraud, and legal workflows.

## How It Works Section

### Three-Step Flow

1. Upload the original file.
2. Provance analyzes multiple forensic signals.
3. Review the verdict, confidence, signal breakdown, and report-ready evidence.

## Why We Are Different

### Pillars

- Explainable evidence
- Multi-signal analysis
- Attribution intelligence
- Report and workflow readiness
- Honest uncertainty handling

### Suggested Copy

Provance does not just flag suspicious media. It shows what signals triggered, what confidence means, and where uncertainty remains so teams can make better decisions.

## Pricing Preview

Show only high-level tiers early:

- Trial
- Pro
- Team
- Enterprise

Do not overcommit pricing before validation if the package is still evolving.

## FAQ Topics

- How accurate is it?
- Does it support images or video?
- What happens if the result is uncertain?
- Can I use it through an API?
- Is this suitable for legal or investigative workflows?

## Page Rules

- lead with trust, not hype
- use real workflow language, not generic AI language
- avoid exaggerated accuracy claims
- show uncertainty as strength, not weakness

## Recommended Pages After Homepage

- Product
- Solutions
- Pricing
- Sample Report
- Methodology
- API / Docs
- Security
- Blog

## Final Recommendation

Build the landing page early, even before full product launch.

It should function as:

- a credibility layer
- a conversion layer
- a category-defining asset
