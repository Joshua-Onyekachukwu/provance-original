# Investor Pitch Deck Outline

## Deck Narrative

The deck should present Provance as a trust infrastructure company, not a novelty AI detection app.

## Slide Outline

### 1. Title

- Provance
- tagline: explainable media verification for the AI-content era

### 2. Problem

- synthetic media is scaling faster than trust infrastructure
- existing tools are opaque and operationally weak

### 3. Why Now

- generative media quality is rising
- regulation, platform pressure, and public trust concerns are increasing
- legal and enterprise workflows need defensible tooling now

### 4. Solution

- explainable verification platform
- evidence-backed verdicts
- attribution and reporting workflows

### 5. Product Demo

- upload
- result
- evidence summary
- sample report
- API view

### 6. Why We Win

- explainability
- attribution intelligence
- evidence and workflow focus
- compounding fingerprint moat

### 7. Market

- verification, fraud prevention, trust and safety, legal evidence, enterprise API

### 8. Business Model

- subscriptions
- API usage
- enterprise contracts
- premium reports

### 9. Go-To-Market

- pilot design partners
- credibility-led content
- developer self-serve
- enterprise expansion

- pilot conversations
- waitlist or demand signals

### 11. Roadmap

- image MVP

### 12. Competition

## Data Room Structure

- corporate docs
- product docs
- technical architecture
- benchmark and methodology
- financial model
- legal docs
- customer / pilot evidence

## Valuation Assumption Guidance

- base initial fundraising around milestone credibility, not inflated enterprise claims
- justify valuation with market urgency, product differentiation, and evidence of technical feasibility

## Investor Outreach Plan

- target angels and pre-seed funds aligned with AI infrastructure, trust and safety, legal tech, and African or global frontier founders
- prioritize warm intros and thesis-fit investors
