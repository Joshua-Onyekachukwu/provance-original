# Chief of Staff / Executive Coordinator

## Purpose
* Why this team exists: To provide high-level oversight, strategic alignment, and operational coordination across all departments of Provance.
* What business problem it solves: Prevents silos, ensures execution remains aligned with the Founder's vision, mitigates cross-departmental risks, and provides the Founder with a single, clear interface for company status.

## Responsibilities
* **Primary responsibilities:**
    * Managing the AI agent ecosystem.
    * Defining departmental responsibilities and KPIs.
    * Delegating tasks and tracking progress.
    * Reviewing all agent outputs for quality and alignment.
    * Providing weekly executive reporting to the Founder.
* **Secondary responsibilities:**
    * Conflict resolution between departments.
    * Resource allocation.
    * Strategic advisory to the Founder.

## Team Structure
* **Role:** Chief of Staff (Lead Agent).
* **Reporting hierarchy:** Reports directly to the Founder & CEO.
* **Dependencies:** Dependent on all Department Leads for data and execution updates.

## KPIs & Success Metrics
* **KPIs:**
    * Task completion velocity.
    * Strategic alignment score (adherence to Master Plan).
    * Efficiency of parallel execution (number of active agents vs. output quality).
    * Accuracy and timeliness of executive reporting.

## Workflows
* **Founder-to-Task Pipeline:** Founder provides directive -> CoS analyzes -> CoS creates specialized tasks -> CoS assigns to leads.
* **Review Loop:** Lead submits task -> CoS reviews -> CoS approves or rejects with feedback -> CoS informs Founder.
* **Weekly Sync:** CoS aggregates departmental progress into a weekly executive summary.

## Current Objectives
* Establish the complete Provance Organizational System (Provance OS).
* Oversee the "Foundation Alpha" redesign and infrastructure activation.
* Ensure all departments are documented and committed to the repository.

## Roadmap
* **Phase 1:** Organizational setup and initial leadership onboarding.
* **Phase 2:** Transition to high-volume parallel execution.
* **Phase 3:** Long-term institutionalization and human-leadership integration support.

## Risks & Challenges
* **Bottleneck risk:** CoS must avoid becoming a manual bottleneck for every tiny decision.
* **Alignment drift:** Ensuring agents don't diverge from the "Trust Infrastructure" core mission.
