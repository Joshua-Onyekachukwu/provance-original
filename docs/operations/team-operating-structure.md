# Team Operating Structure

## Operating Principle

Run Provance as a product-and-trust company. Every team function should reinforce quality, credibility, and defensibility.

## Organizational Structure

### Founding Layer

- Founder / CEO / Product Architect
- Founding ML Engineer
- Founding Full-Stack Engineer
- Founding Backend / Platform Engineer

### Product And Experience

- Product Design
- Technical Product Management

### Trust And Operations

- QA / Test
- Security / Compliance advisor
- Customer success / solutions

### Growth And Commercial

- Founder-led sales initially
- Later GTM lead
- Content / thought leadership support

## Department Responsibilities

### Product

- strategy
- roadmap
- customer discovery
- requirements

### Engineering

- platform delivery
- infrastructure
- application reliability
- security implementation

### ML / Intelligence

- dataset program
- model training
- evaluation
- attribution and calibration

### Design

- UX systems
- product flows
- brand consistency

### Operations

- release readiness
- support process
- internal tooling
- governance tracking

## Role Descriptions

### Founder / Product Architect

- owns strategy, scope, GTM narrative, investor communication, and product decisions

### ML Engineer

- owns model experimentation, benchmarking, calibration, and intelligence roadmap

### Full-Stack Engineer

- owns end-user product workflows across frontend and backend integration

### Backend / Platform Engineer

- owns orchestration, data systems, security, deployability, and observability

### Product Designer

- owns trust-centered UX and design system governance

## KPIs

### Product KPIs

- activation
- repeat usage
- report usage
- pilot satisfaction

### Engineering KPIs

- deployment reliability
- mean time to recovery
- queue stability
- uptime

### ML KPIs

- benchmark performance
- false positive rate
- calibration quality
- drift detection time

### Business KPIs

- MRR
- conversion rate
- retention
- enterprise pipeline value

## Performance Framework

- tie success to outcome metrics, not raw feature output
- review roadmap monthly
- review product quality and incidents weekly
- review benchmark and model drift on a regular cadence

## Hiring Plan

### First 3 Hires

1. ML engineer
2. full-stack engineer
3. backend/platform engineer

### Next 3 Hires

1. product designer
2. customer success / solutions
3. GTM or technical sales support
