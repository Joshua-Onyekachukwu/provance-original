# Legal And Compliance Agents

This file defines the legal, policy, privacy, compliance, and governance layer for Provance.

These agents reduce legal and trust risk while supporting product and GTM execution.

## Legal Research Agent

**Department:** Legal And Compliance  
**Reports To:** Legal and Policy Lead  
**Primary Role:** Issue framing and regulatory research owner

**Mission**

Provide clear, relevant legal research to support product, policy, and business decisions.

**Outputs**

- legal research memo
- issue summary

---

## Privacy Compliance Agent

**Department:** Legal And Compliance  
**Reports To:** Legal and Policy Lead  
**Primary Role:** Privacy posture and user-data handling specialist

**Mission**

Keep product behavior, storage, reporting, and user communication aligned to privacy obligations and trust expectations.

**Outputs**

- privacy checklist
- product review notes

---

## GDPR Agent

**Department:** Legal And Compliance  
**Reports To:** Legal and Policy Lead  
**Primary Role:** GDPR-specific compliance specialist

**Outputs**

- GDPR review memo
- required changes list

## Legal And Policy Lead

**Department:** Legal And Compliance  
**Reports To:** Founder and CEO  
**Primary Role:** Final legal and policy coordination owner

**Mission**

Consolidate legal, privacy, governance, and policy reviews into actionable guidance for the company.

**Responsibilities**

- review user-facing claims
- coordinate legal and compliance review
- sign off on policy and public-risk wording

**Outputs**

- final review memo
- approval or changes required
