# Naming And Brand Strategy Report

## Current Working Name: Provance

## Assessment

### Strengths

- feels premium and serious
- broad enough to scale beyond one product feature
- suitable for trust, governance, or infrastructure positioning

### Weaknesses

- meaning is not immediately obvious
- can be confused with provenance, which may be helpful conceptually but not always clearly intentional
- current source materials use VerifAI more explicitly for the product idea

### Brand Potential

Good company-level potential if positioned around trust, provenance, and verification.

### Memorability

Moderately strong. It is distinctive, but it may need deliberate storytelling to anchor meaning.

### Scalability

Strong as a company brand because it is not boxed into a single use case.

## Recommendation

Use a two-layer brand structure:

- Company brand: Provance
- Product brand: VerifAI or a refined product name in the verification category

This preserves strategic breadth while keeping the initial product immediately legible.

## Alternative Names

### Provenric

- Meaning: provenance plus forensic rigor
- Positioning: premium trust infrastructure
- Brand story: built for evidence and verification
- Domain guidance: pursue `.com` if available, otherwise consider a short trusted TLD
- Trademark note: run standard class review before commitment

### Vericust

- Meaning: verification plus trust
- Positioning: enterprise verification platform
- Brand story: confident decisions backed by evidence
- Domain guidance: prioritize exact-match domain where possible
- Trademark note: likely more protectable than highly descriptive terms

### Evidentra

- Meaning: evidence-driven platform identity
- Positioning: forensic and legal-grade verification
- Brand story: turns signal into defensible evidence
- Domain guidance: acquire exact-match or a clean brand domain variant
- Trademark note: verify similarity against evidence-related SaaS brands

### Truvola

- Meaning: trust with velocity and modernity
- Positioning: scalable trust infrastructure
- Brand story: helps teams verify fast without sacrificing rigor
- Domain guidance: seek clean `.com` or strong brand domain alternative
- Trademark note: assess phonetic similarity in software classes

### Forensiqo

- Meaning: modernized forensic identity
- Positioning: premium forensic intelligence brand
- Brand story: purpose-built for high-scrutiny verification workflows
- Domain guidance: exact-match preferred
- Trademark note: review similarity with existing forensic and security brands

## Domain Availability Recommendations

- check exact `.com` first
- if unavailable, evaluate high-quality alternatives only if brand remains strong
- avoid awkward hyphenated domains for the company brand
- reserve primary and product-specific domains early once a direction is chosen

## Trademark Considerations

- conduct jurisdiction-specific searches before launch
- prioritize names that are distinctive rather than descriptive
- secure company and product marks separately if using a two-brand system

## Final Recommendation

Keep `Provance` as the umbrella identity unless domain or trademark issues block it. For the product, either keep `VerifAI` temporarily for clarity or select a stronger product name once positioning is finalized.
