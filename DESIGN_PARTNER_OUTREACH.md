## Outreach Strategy Overview

### Narrative Architecture

Every outreach email follows the same structure:
1. **Hook** — Reference the recipient's known work or challenge they face
2. **Problem** — Current "AI detectors" fail them (opaque scores, no report artifact, no courtroom readiness)
3. **Provance Solution** — Evidence-first forensic verification with a downloadable, citable report
4. **Explicit Offer** — Design partner access: free highest-tier for 6 months + direct founding team access
5. **Call to Action** — 15-minute intro call + attached Sample Report PDF

### Attachments for Every Send

- ✅ `provance.pdf` — The Sample Forensic Report (artifact that proves our value)
- → *Hosted at: https://provance.ai/sample-report* (when deployed)

## Key Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| **Low open rate** | A/B test subject lines; use warmer intros; send from personal address |
| **"Not interested" replies** | Ask why — use as market research; adjust offer or positioning |
| **Product not ready for demo** | Focus on Sample Report artifact + methodology; don't promise live dashboard if not stable |
| **Design partner wants guarantees** | Be honest about product stage; emphasize influence on roadmap |
| **Competitor poaching** | Differentiate by report artifact + evidence-first approach; share Veracity Language doc |
