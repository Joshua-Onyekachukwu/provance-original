# SAFE Template And Term Sheet Brief

## Important Note

This is a founder briefing document, not legal advice.

Use a lawyer-reviewed SAFE before signing anything.

## Recommended Instrument

For Provance's first real outside round, the recommended instrument is:

- **post-money SAFE**

Why:

- easier to close than a priced round
- simpler than a note
- clearer dilution visibility than older uncapped structures

## Recommended Current Terms

### Main Pre-Seed Ask

- amount: `$650k`
- valuation cap: `$6M post-money`
- no discount required if demand is healthy
- MFN only if appropriate and counsel approves

### Acceptable Range

- lower bound: `$500k` at `$5M cap`
- stretch range: `$750k-$1M` at `$6.5M-$7M cap`

## Terms To Decide With Counsel

- valuation cap
- discount, if any
- pro rata rights
- MFN clause
- conversion events
- governing law

## Founder-Friendly SAFE Structure

### Prefer

- one consistent post-money SAFE form
- clear cap
- limited special rights

### Avoid

- uncapped SAFE
- stacking multiple odd side letters
- investor-specific promises that distort later rounds

## Template Clause Checklist

- parties
- purchase amount
- valuation cap
- discount rate if used
- equity financing conversion
- liquidity event treatment
- dissolution event treatment
- representations
- governing law

## Operational Rules

- track every SAFE in the cap table immediately
- do not sign verbal side promises
- keep signed copies in the legal folder and data room
- model dilution before every new SAFE

## Suggested Close Strategy

- set a target close window
- use one standard document
- avoid bespoke edits unless strategically necessary

## Rule Of Thumb

Raise enough on the SAFE to reach proof.

Do not try to solve long-term scale with a pre-seed instrument.
