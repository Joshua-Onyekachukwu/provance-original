# Investor Pitch Deck Outline (Revised — Seed Round $2M–$5M)

**Revision:** 2.0
**Date:** 2026-06-25
**Author:** Business Strategy Lead

## Deck Narrative

Provance is **trust infrastructure for synthetic media** — not a detection tool. The deck should make the category undeniable and the business model investable.

## Slide Outline

### 1. Title
- Provance: Trust Infrastructure for Synthetic Media
- Seed Round: $2M–$5M
- Tagline: Explainable verification. Forensic evidence. Enterprise workflow.

### 2. The Problem — The Four Failures of AI Detectors
- Opaque scores (not defensible)
- False certainty (never say "I don't know")
- No artifact (no PDF, no evidence appendix)
- No self-serve (enterprise sales calls for everyone)

### 3. Why Now — Three Forces Converge
- Regulatory mandate (EU AI Act, US state laws, C2PA)
- Professional demand (newsrooms, law firms, security teams)
- Technical maturity (multi-signal analysis production-ready)

### 4. The Solution — Defensible Verification Workflow
- Upload → Analyze (5 signals) → Verdict (6 classes) → Export (PDF report)
- Verdict: Authentic → Likely Authentic → Inconclusive → Suspicious → Likely Synthetic → Synthetic
- Report: Cover page, executive summary, evidence appendix, metadata, methodology, chain-of-custody

### 5. Product Demo
- Upload interface (drag-drop, URL)
- Progressive signal analysis (real-time status)
- Verdict & evidence breakdown (6-class, color-coded)
- Report preview and download
- API response view

### 6. Why We Win — The Trust Gap
- Epistemology: Explainable evidence vs. opaque scores
- Admissibility: Forensic PDF vs. screenshot
- Honesty: "Inconclusive" as first-class verdict vs. false certainty
- Access: Self-serve Trial → Pro ($49) vs. enterprise-only

### 7. Market Opportunity
- TAM: $3.5B (synthetic media detection + digital forensics + trust & safety)
- ICP priority: 1. Journalism 2. Legal 3. Enterprise T&S 4. Developers
- GTM: credibility-led content → founder-led pilots → product-led self-serve

### 8. Business Model
- 4-tier SaaS: Trial (free/14d) → Pro ($49/mo) → Team ($249/mo) → Enterprise ($2K–$10K+/mo)
- API-only: Lite ($0.05/req) → Pro ($199/mo)
- Annual discount: 20%
- Unit economics: LTV:CAC 3.5:1 – 39.8:1 per tier

### 9. Financial Projections (3-Year)
- Y1 ARR: $632K → Y2: $2.3M → Y3: $7.3M
- Gross margin: 65% → 74% → 79%
- Breakeven: Month 22–24
- Capital efficiency: $0.42 burn per $1 ARR by end of Y2

### 10. Traction (June 2026)
- Live: Website (5 pages), design system, pricing, product/methodology pages
- Defined: Veracity Language (6 classes), Report Flywheel, revenue model, investor narrative
- In progress: Interactive dashboard, video processing, design partner program
- Pipeline: 10–30 design partners targeted across 4 segments

### 11. Competitive Landscape
- Hive: Scale API, no report artifact
- Sensity: Enterprise monitoring, $50K+ entry, no self-serve
- Reality Defender: Channel security, $100K+ entry, compliance focus
- Deepware: Open-source, limited signals
- Truepic: C2PA capture, different problem
- Provance uniquely: affordable entry + evidence-first workflow

### 12. Technology & Moat
- Multi-signal: Generative fingerprint (35%), pixel/frequency (25%), metadata (20%), compression (10%), C2PA (10%)
- 4-layer moat: Data loop → Attribution graph → Workflow lock-in → Trust credibility

### 13. Team Plan
- Core: Product architect, ML engineer, full-stack engineer, platform engineer, designer, GTM lead
- Seed hires: 5–8 FTEs in first 12 months

### 14. The Ask
- $2M–$5M seed (post-money SAFE or priced round)
- Use: Engineering 45%, Design/Product 15%, GTM 18%, Infra 10%, Legal 6%, Ops 6%
- Milestones: Dashboard beta (Q3), 10–30 design partners (Q3–Q4), video engine (Q4), API pilots (Q1), $500K+ ARR

### 15. Vision
- "When stakes are real, Provance is the workflow for deciding what's authentic, synthetic, or uncertain."
- Phase roadmap: Foundation → Scale → Ecosystem → Infrastructure
